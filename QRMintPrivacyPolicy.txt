PRIVACY POLICY — QRMint
Last updated: April 1, 2026

================================================================================

1. INTRODUCTION

This Privacy Policy describes how QRMint ("the App", "we", "our") handles
information when you use our Android application. We are committed to protecting
your privacy and being transparent about our practices.

QRMint is designed with a privacy-first approach. The core functionality of the
App — QR code generation and scanning — operates entirely on your device without
sending any data to external servers.

--------------------------------------------------------------------------------

2. INFORMATION WE DO NOT COLLECT

QRMint does NOT collect, store, transmit, or share:
• The text you type or paste into the App
• The content of QR codes you generate
• The content of QR codes you scan
• Your clipboard contents
• Photos or images from your device
• Contacts, messages, or call logs
• Your location
• Any personal information

All QR code generation and scanning is performed 100% on your device. No text,
images, or QR code content ever leaves your phone through our App.

--------------------------------------------------------------------------------

3. INFORMATION THAT MAY BE COLLECTED BY THIRD PARTIES

The App uses the following third-party services, each with their own privacy
policies:

a) Google AdMob (Advertising)
   The App displays banner advertisements provided by Google AdMob. Google may
   collect and use data for personalized advertising as described in Google's
   Privacy Policy. This may include:
   • Advertising ID
   • Device information (model, OS version)
   • General location (based on IP address)
   • Ad interaction data

   You can opt out of personalized ads in your Android device settings:
   Settings → Google → Ads → Opt out of Ads Personalization.

   Google's Privacy Policy: https://policies.google.com/privacy

b) Google Firebase Analytics
   The App may use Firebase Analytics to collect anonymous usage data such as:
   • App opens and session duration
   • Screen views
   • Device type and operating system version
   • Country (based on IP address)

   This data is aggregated and anonymous. It does not include any text you type,
   QR codes you generate, or content you scan.

   Firebase Privacy: https://firebase.google.com/support/privacy

c) Google ML Kit (Barcode Scanning)
   The App uses Google ML Kit for on-device barcode scanning. ML Kit processes
   camera frames entirely on your device. No camera images or scanned data are
   sent to Google servers.

   ML Kit Privacy: https://developers.google.com/ml-kit/terms

--------------------------------------------------------------------------------

4. PERMISSIONS

The App requests the following Android permissions:

• CAMERA — Required for QR code scanning. Camera access is only used when you
  open the Receive/Scan screen. No photos are taken or stored.

• INTERNET — Required for loading advertisements (Google AdMob) and anonymous
  analytics (Firebase). Not used for transmitting your content.

• READ_MEDIA_IMAGES (Android 13+) / READ_EXTERNAL_STORAGE (Android 12 and
  below) — Required to display saved QR code images in the Saved QR library.

• WRITE_EXTERNAL_STORAGE (Android 9 and below) — Required to save QR code
  images to your device gallery on older Android versions.

--------------------------------------------------------------------------------

5. DATA STORAGE

QR code images you choose to save are stored locally on your device in the
Pictures/QRMint folder. These files remain entirely on your device and are not
uploaded or synced anywhere.

The App stores a small amount of preference data locally on your device:
• Theme setting (Dark / Light / System)
• Onboarding completion flag

This data is stored using Android SharedPreferences and never leaves your device.

--------------------------------------------------------------------------------

6. DATA SHARING

We do not sell, trade, rent, or share your personal information with any third
parties. The only data shared with third parties is the anonymous data collected
by Google AdMob and Firebase Analytics as described in Section 3 above.

--------------------------------------------------------------------------------

7. CHILDREN'S PRIVACY

QRMint does not knowingly collect any personal information from children under
the age of 13. The App's content is suitable for all ages. The advertisements
shown are provided by Google AdMob and comply with Google's policies regarding
ads shown to children.

--------------------------------------------------------------------------------

8. SECURITY

Since QRMint processes all QR code content locally on your device, your data
never traverses the internet through our App. There are no servers to breach,
no databases to hack, and no cloud storage to compromise. Your text stays on
your phone.
