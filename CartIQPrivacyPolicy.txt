PRIVACY POLICY — CartIQ
Last updated: January 2025

1. INTRODUCTION
CartIQ ("we", "us", "our") is a smart shopping list and grocery management
application for Android. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use,
and protect your information when you use DataIQ Technologies' CartIQ mobile
application (the "App").

By using CartIQ you agree to the practices described in this policy.

2. INFORMATION WE COLLECT

2.1 Information You Provide
• Account data — name, email address, and profile photo when you sign in
  with Google.
• Shopping data — shopping lists, items, quantities, prices, categories, and
  notes you create within the App.
• Chat messages — text you enter in the AI chatbot feature (processed
  entirely on-device).

2.2 Information Collected Automatically
• Device information — device model, OS version, app version, and unique
  device identifiers for crash reporting.
• Usage analytics — screens visited, features used, and interaction patterns
  collected through Firebase Analytics. These events do not contain personal
  shopping data.
• Crash reports — stack traces and device state at the time of a crash,
  collected through Firebase Crashlytics.

2.3 Information We Do NOT Collect
• We do NOT access your contacts, call logs, SMS, or location.
• We do NOT record audio, video, or use the camera beyond barcode scanning.
• Barcode scans are processed on-device and are not transmitted to servers.
• AI/ML models run entirely on your device. No shopping data is sent to
  external AI services.

3. HOW WE USE YOUR INFORMATION
• To provide and improve the App's features.
• To sync your shopping data across devices via Google Drive backup
  (only when you explicitly enable backup).
• To send restock reminders and notification alerts you configure.
• To display relevant advertisements through Google AdMob (non-personalized
  ads for users who do not consent to personalized ads).
• To analyze aggregate usage patterns and fix bugs.

4. DATA STORAGE AND SECURITY
• Shopping data is stored locally on your device using an encrypted Room
  database.
• Cloud backups (Google Drive) are encrypted with AES-256 before upload.
  Only you can decrypt your backup files.
• Authentication tokens are stored securely using Android Keystore.
• We use industry-standard security measures to protect data in transit
  (TLS 1.2+) and at rest.

5. THIRD-PARTY SERVICES
The App integrates the following third-party services, each governed by their
own privacy policies:
• Firebase (Google) — Analytics, Authentication, Cloud Firestore
  https://firebase.google.com/support/privacy
• Google AdMob — Advertising
  https://policies.google.com/privacy
• Google Drive API — Cloud backup
  https://developers.google.com/drive/api/guides/about-auth
• Google Play In-App Review and In-App Update APIs

6. AI AND ML MODELS
• CartIQ offers optional on-device ML models (~250 KB) to enhance intent
  recognition, item categorization, and fuzzy name matching.
• These models are downloaded from our servers and run entirely on your
  device. No user data is sent to our servers for AI processing.
• You can enable or disable ML models at any time from Settings → AI Models.
• When ML models are disabled, the App uses keyword-based fallback logic.

7. ADVERTISING
• CartIQ displays ads via Google AdMob.
• Free-tier users see banner and interstitial ads. Pro users enjoy an
  ad-free experience.
• You may opt out of personalized advertising through your device's
  "Opt out of Ads Personalization" setting.

8. DATA SHARING
• We do NOT sell, rent, or trade your personal information.
• We share data only with the third-party service providers listed above,
  solely for the purposes described in this policy.
• We may disclose data if required by law or to protect our legal rights.

9. DATA RETENTION AND DELETION
• Your local data remains on your device until you uninstall the App or
  clear its data.
• Cloud backups on Google Drive can be deleted at any time from your
  Google Drive account.
• You can delete your account data by signing out and clearing app data
  from Android Settings.
• To request complete data deletion, contact us at the email below.

10. CHILDREN'S PRIVACY
CartIQ is not directed to children under 13. We do not knowingly collect
personal information from children under 13. If you believe we have
collected information from a child, please contact us for immediate
deletion.

11. YOUR RIGHTS
Depending on your jurisdiction you may have the right to:
• Access, correct, or delete your personal data.
• Object to or restrict certain processing.
• Data portability — export your shopping data via Settings → Export Data.
• Withdraw consent for analytics or personalized ads at any time.

12. CHANGES TO THIS POLICY
We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. The "Last updated" date
at the top indicates the latest revision. Continued use of the App after
changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
