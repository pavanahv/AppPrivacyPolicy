🔒 BookWise Privacy Policy
Last Updated: April 2, 2026

Effective Date: April 2, 2026

TL;DR: BookWise processes everything on your device. We do not collect, store, or transmit your personal data, book content, notes, or chat history to any server. Period.

1. Introduction
BookWise ("the App") is developed by Pavan ("we", "us", "our"). This privacy policy explains how we handle your data when you use the App.

We are committed to protecting your privacy. The App is designed from the ground up to keep your data on your device.

2. Data We Do NOT Collect
We do not collect, access, or transmit:

📚 Your book files or their contents

💬 Your chat questions or AI responses

📝 Your notes or bookmarks

🎤 Your voice recordings or speech-to-text data

📷 Your scanned images or photos

👤 Your name, email, or any personal information

📍 Your location data

3. On-Device AI Processing
BookWise uses AI models that run entirely on your device using ONNX Runtime. This means:

✅ All question-answering happens locally

✅ All summaries are generated locally

✅ No book content is sent to any server

✅ No internet is required after initial model download (~112 MB one-time)

4. Permissions We Request
The App requests the following Android permissions, each with a specific purpose:

Storage / Media Access: To import book files (PDF, EPUB, TXT) from your device. Files are copied locally and never uploaded.

Camera: To photograph book pages for the Scan Book feature. Photos are processed on-device and never leave your phone.

Microphone: For voice notes and voice input via Android's built-in Speech-to-Text. Audio is processed on-device only.

Internet: Used solely for: (1) one-time AI model download, (2) anonymous Firebase Analytics, (3) checking for app updates. No personal data is transmitted.

Notifications: To show progress when indexing books in the background.

Vibrate: For haptic feedback on user interactions.

5. Analytics
We use Firebase Analytics to collect anonymous, aggregated usage statistics (e.g., which features are popular, crash reports). Firebase Analytics:

Does NOT collect your book content or personal data

Uses anonymized identifiers that cannot identify you personally

Helps us improve the app experience

You can opt out of analytics by disabling usage data sharing in your device's Google Settings.

6. Data Storage
All app data is stored locally on your device using:

Room Database — for books, notes, bookmarks, and chat history

SharedPreferences / DataStore — for app settings (theme, etc.)

Internal Storage — for imported book files and AI models

You can delete all data by clearing the app's data in Android Settings or by uninstalling the App.

7. Third-Party Services
The App uses the following third-party services:

Firebase Analytics (Google) — anonymous usage analytics. Firebase Privacy

Google Play In-App Updates — to check for app updates

Google Play In-App Review — to prompt for reviews within the app

8. Open-Source Licenses
BookWise uses the following open-source models and libraries. All AI models run entirely on-device:

ONNX Runtime (Microsoft): On-device AI inference engine.

all-MiniLM-L6-v2 (Microsoft): Sentence embeddings for semantic search.

DistilBERT QA (Hugging Face): Question answering model.

Cross-Encoder MiniLM: Relevance reranking.

ML Kit Text Recognition (Google): On-device OCR for scanning.

PDFBox for Android (Apache): PDF parsing and generation.

Lottie (Airbnb): Animations.

Glide (Bump Technologies): Image loading.

9. Children's Privacy
The App is suitable for all ages. We do not knowingly collect data from children or any users. Since all processing is on-device, no personal data from any user is transmitted.

10. Data Security
Since your data never leaves your device, security is inherently maintained by Android's app sandbox. The App uses internal storage that is inaccessible to other apps.

11. Changes to This Policy
